Squid Game: Rules & Regulations

Code of Conduct

  • Physical violence, abusive language, and cheating are strictly prohibited.

Dress Code

  • Participants must wear the provided uniforms and identification badges during the event.

Participation Fee

    Any entry fee must be paid in full before participation.

Game-Specific Rules

  • Game Format: The event will consist of multiple rounds inspired by traditional games (e.g., Tug of War, Red Light Green Light), adapted for safety.
  • Elimination: Once eliminated, participants must leave the game area immediately and may not interfere with ongoing games.
  • Safety Protocols: All activities will use safe, non-harmful equipment, and professional supervision will be provided.
  • Restart/Appeals: If a game is disrupted due to unforeseen circumstances, it may be restarted at the discretion of the organizers.

Behavior and Disqualification

  • Integrity: Any participant caught cheating or sabotaging others will be disqualified.
  • Cooperation: Participants must follow the instructions of the game moderators and staff.
  • Teamwork: For team-based games, participants must cooperate with their assigned team.

Legal and Financial

  • Prize Money: The total prize amount and distribution process will be announced before the start of the event.
  • No Refunds: Entry fees are non-refundable unless the event is canceled by the organizers.
  • Photography and Media: By participating, players consent to being photographed or recorded for promotional purposes.
